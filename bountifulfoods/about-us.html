About Us

Bobby Bountiful moved to Carlsbad, California and realized there were no good smoothie shops in the area
and so he often had to go to Oceanside to get a good smoothie and that just wasn't sustainable because of
the traffic. So Bobby went about scheming and planning to create the best smoothie shop San Diego county had
ever seen. He made plans, deals and eventually he opened his smoothie shop, Bountiful Foods. People rushed
to get smoothies and celebrated that they didn't have to go to Oceanside anymore to get there smoothies. A
fun fact, I actually do have a friend from Carlsbad and when I told her I was making this website she told me that
there really weren't much options for good smoothie shops in Carlsbad so there's definitely a business oppurtunity here.
